Private Music Teacher

Juliet Gjone

Lessons at Afternoon Arpeggios are shaped around each individual student, their goals at their own pace and what genuinely excites them about music. All ages are welcome. Whether you are a complete beginner or working towards AMEB examinations, every session is designed to feel encouraging and enjoyable. Teaching happens from a cozy home studio in Hobart, with a couch where parents and family are always welcome to sit in on the lesson, in the company of a very friendly studio cat.

Terms & Conditions

Cancellations by students

A minimum of 24 hours notice is required. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice will be charged at the full lesson rate.

Cancellations by Juliet

In the rare event Juliet needs to cancel, you will receive at least 24 hours notice by email. No charge will apply. This is most likely due to a conflicting performance.

Specific weeks away

You can cancel specific weeks from My Schedule in your account. No payment will be taken and your slot will be temporarily released for that week only. Your recurring slot resumes the following week.

Subscription pause

You may pause your subscription at any time. Warning: pausing releases your reserved slot and it may be taken by another student before you return.

Family bookings

One account, one weekly payment. Students within a family booking can cancel independently without affecting the others.

Studio closures

The studio is closed for approximately three weeks over Christmas and New Year from December 21 to January 3. No fees are charged during this period. The studio remains open on all Tasmanian public holidays and school holidays.

Stopping lessons

End your enrolment from My Account. Your account will be deactivated. You can reactivate at any time by logging back in.

Payment

All payments are processed via Stripe. Card, Apple Pay, and Google Pay are all secured through Stripe (256-bit SSL, PCI DSS compliant). Your card is saved but only charged after each lesson passes. No charge is made upfront at enrolment.

Privacy

Personal information is used solely for scheduling, billing, and communication. Encrypted and stored securely. Not shared with third parties except Stripe for payment processing.
